Peer-reviewed Articles

Fertility, Household Size and Poverty in Nepal

François Libois & Vincent Somville

World Development, 2018

We study the relationship between fertility, household size, and poverty in Nepal, using panel data. Our findings suggest that population control policies may have unintended consequences on household welfare.

François Libois, Jean-Marie Baland, Nicolas Delbart & Subhrendu Pattanayak

This paper explores the factors behind the success of community forest management in Nepal, focusing on institutional design and local governance.

Firewood Collections and Economic Growth in Rural Nepal 1995-2010: Evidence from a Household Panel

Jean-Marie Baland, François Libois & Dilip Mookherjee

Working Paper, University of Namur

This study examines the impact of economic growth on firewood collection and forest management practices in rural Nepal.
